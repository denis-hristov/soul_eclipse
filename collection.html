Material and care

All Soul Eclipse pieces are made from soft cotton blends chosen for daily wear. Prints are created using high quality techniques to preserve the dotted detail and contrast.

  • Wash inside out at 30 degrees Celsius on a gentle cycle.
  • Do not bleach. Avoid strong chemicals and harsh detergents.
  • Line dry if possible. Avoid high heat in the dryer.
  • Iron inside out on low heat, never directly on the print.
