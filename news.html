News and drops

Here you can find current and upcoming drops, events and short updates from the Soul Eclipse universe.

December 10, 2025 · Drop

New drop: "Broken Halo" capsule

The "Broken Halo" capsule focuses on loyalty, pressure and the silent work of becoming a better version of yourself. The capsule includes one tee and one hoodie, both in black with a cracked halo print made from dots. Pieces are limited and will not be restocked.

Available online in the Collection section from December 15. Members of our mailing list get early access twenty four hours before the public release.

November 1, 2025 · Event

Pop up night with Silent Frame Studio

We are teaming up with Silent Frame Studio for a one night pop up event. You can see the full Soul Eclipse collection live, try pieces on and take photos in a simple black and white set.

The event will take place at Silent Frame Studio on November 20 from 18:00 to 22:00. Entry is free, but the space is small, so come early if you want to stay longer.

September 15, 2025 · Update

Shattered Light crewneck sold out

The Shattered Light crewneck is now officially sold out. There will be no restock, but a related design is planned for a future drop, inspired by the idea that light can break and still exist in new forms.

If you missed this one, do not worry. Every drop brings a new way to wear your story.

July 1, 2025 · Collaboration

Upcoming collaboration with local illustrator

We are working with a local illustrator to create a small run of designs that mix Soul Eclipse dots with hand drawn lines. The goal is to show how two different styles can live on the same fabric without one of them disappearing.

More details and previews will be shared in the Gallery section and on our social channels soon.
