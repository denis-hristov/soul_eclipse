What makes Soul Eclipse different

Limited series

Every design is released in a limited number of pieces and never repeated. What you wear is a moment in time that will not come back.

Tattoo-like symbolism

Prints are abstract and built from dots. From afar you see the full image, up close – the details of your story, like ink on fabric instead of skin.

Black, white and your soul between

We work only in the black & white spectrum to highlight the contrast between who you show on the outside and who you are on the inside.

Who Soul Eclipse is for

For young men and women between 16 and 28 who wear clothes as an extension of themselves – not just for a picture, but as a symbol of strength, honesty, confidence, respect and quiet kindness inside.

These pieces are not for the gym or formal events. They are for the city, the streets, late-night talks, long walks and the moments when you are simply you.

Every print is a story

Just like a tattoo on skin, the print on your clothes can carry meaning. Behind each design there is a story about inner battles, about the strength to stay gentle, and about looking solid on the outside while still being built from pieces within.
